Meat Pierogi

Pierogi are filled dumplings made by wrapping unleavened dough around a savoury or sweet filling and cooked in boiling water. They are often then pan-fried before serving.

Pierogi are most often associated with the cuisine of Central and Eastern European nations.

ingredients:

  • flour
  • meat
  • spices
  • onion

Steps:

  1. make a dough
  2. roll the dough into small balls
  3. flatten them
  4. add a bit of the meat mixed with spices
  5. close the flattend dough into a pierogi shape
  6. boil for 15 min and frie the diced onions until caramelised
